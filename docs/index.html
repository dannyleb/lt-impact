CRE Assignment Matcher

Multi-factor scoring engine that ranks CREs against customer accounts across Skills (40%), Region (25%), Capacity (20%), and Tier Fit (15%). Expanded search beyond Premium Plus to all ~35,000 GitHub enterprise accounts for proactive assignment planning.

Swap Suggestions Engine

Two-level scoring system evaluating account swappability (CHIP health, stability trend, risk status, complexity) and CRE experience (portfolio breadth, complexity exposure, product diversity). Surfaces 137 swappable accounts across 35 CREs with receiving CRE recommendations.
